# Rollback and Rescue Plan

Before any future flash attempt:

- Back up all data.
- Identify the exact stock restore package before flashing.
- Record checksum for the stock restore package.
- Confirm Windows drivers and platform-tools readiness.
- Check fastboot access before flashing.
- Keep the second phone available for communication and lookup.
- Do not lock the bootloader.
- Do not flash if battery is low.
- Keep current firmware data written down: HyperOS 2.0.6.0 UKQEUXM, Android 14 / UKQ1.240624.001.
- Keep exact region notes written down: UKQEUXM.
- Keep the PC and USB cable available until rollback is proven.

This plan intentionally does not include flashing commands.
